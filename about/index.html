Hi! My name is Sean. I make software that helps people express their creativity and share it with the world. Currently, I work as Director of Creative Tools at Madefire. Previously, I worked at Adobe, helping build design tools such as Adobe XD. I have a B.S. in computer science from UCLA and an M.S. in information and computer science from the Arts Computation Engineering program at UC Irvine.

My primary interests fall into the categories of computer graphics, user-interface design, human-computer interaction, functional programming, typography, and the new media arts.

My technical experience is broad, but I tend to specialize in Objective-C, C/C++, and JavaScript/TypeScript.

This site is a personal compendium of various projects and musings. Feel free to browse, marvel, scoff or enjoy!
