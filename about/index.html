Hello! My name is Sean. I make software that helps people share and create. Currently, I work as an Engineering Manager in the Experience Design group at Adobe. I have a B.S. in computer science from UCLA and an M.S. in information and computer science from the Arts Computation Engineering program at UC Irvine.

My primary interests fall into the categories of digital publishing, data visualization, multitouch and embodied interaction, typography, computational linguistics, artificial intelligence, and the new media arts.

My technical experience is broad, but I tend to specialize in Objective-C (Cocoa iOS/OS X), JavaScript, Ruby (Ruby on Rails), and C for Arduino and AVR microcontrollers.

This site is a personal compendium of various projects and musings. Feel free to browse, marvel, scoff or enjoy!
