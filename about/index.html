Hi! My name is Sean. I make software that helps people express and share their creativity. Currently, I work as a Sr. Computer Scientist at Adobe, helping to build Adobe XD. I have a B.S. in computer science from UCLA and an M.S. in information and computer science from the Arts Computation Engineering program at UC Irvine.

My primary interests fall into the categories of computer graphics, data visualization, multitouch and embodied interaction, typography, computational linguistics, and the new media arts.

My technical experience is broad, but I tend to specialize in Objective-C, C/C++, JavaScript, and C for Arduino and AVR microcontrollers.

This site is a personal compendium of various projects and musings. Feel free to browse, marvel, scoff or enjoy!
