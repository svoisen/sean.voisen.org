Hi! My name is Sean. I make software that helps people express their creativity and share it with the world. Currently, I work at Mozilla engineering manager for the team responsible for layout in Firefox. Previously, I spent a good chunk of time at Adobe, helping build design tools such as Adobe XD. I have a B.S. in computer science from UCLA and an M.S. in information and computer science from the Arts Computation Engineering program at UC Irvine.

My primary interests fall into the categories of computer graphics, user-interface design, human-computer interaction, functional programming, typography, and the new media arts.

My technical experience is broad, but I tend to specialize in C/C++, Objective-C, and JavaScript/TypeScript.

This site is a personal compendium of various projects and musings. Feel free to browse, marvel, scoff or enjoy!
