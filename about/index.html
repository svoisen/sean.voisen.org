Hello! My name is Sean. I make software that helps people share and create. Currently, I work as a Senior. Experience Developer at Adobe XD. I have a B.S. in computer science from UCLA and an M.S. in information and computer science from the Arts Computation Engineering program at UC Irvine.

My primary interests fall into the categories of digital publishing, data visualization, ludic design, ubiquitous computing, computational linguistics, artificial intelligence, and the new media arts.

This site is a personal compendium of various projects and musings. Feel free to browse, marvel, scoff or enjoy!
